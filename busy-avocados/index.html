Meet Your Therapist

Hi, I'm Anna 👋

I'm the founder of Busy Avocados OT — a Melbourne-based private paediatric occupational therapy practice dedicated to supporting young children and their families.

My journey began with over five years as a Neuropsychologist in educational settings, followed by a transition into Paediatric OT in Australia in 2018. I hold a Master of Occupational Therapy (Monash University) and a Bachelor of Clinical Psychology (Moscow State University).

I'm deeply committed to lifelong learning and evidence-based practice. Outside of work, you'll find me surfing or spending time outdoors with family and friends.

What I Help With

Services & Support Areas

I work with children and families across a broad range of developmental areas — all delivered in natural, everyday environments where children feel most comfortable.

🛁

Self-Care

Bathing, showering, toileting, dressing, and personal hygiene — building confidence and independence in daily routines.

✏️

Fine Motor Skills

Strengthening small muscle groups for writing, cutting, drawing, and precise object manipulation.

🏫

School Readiness

Preparing children for a smooth and successful transition into school life through targeted skill development.

🧩

Play Skills

Enhancing play skills that are essential for cognitive, social and emotional development.

😌

Emotional Regulation

Teaching children strategies for understanding, expressing and managing their emotions effectively.

🤝

Social Skills

Fostering positive social interactions, communication and the ability to make and maintain friendships.

🍽️

Eating & Feeding

Supportive strategies for picky eaters, mealtime participation and developing healthy feeding habits.

🌙

Sleep Hygiene

Addressing factors that affect restful sleep and supporting healthy, consistent bedtime routines.

🧠

Sensory Processing

Understanding and addressing sensory sensitivities that affect participation in everyday activities.

📄

Functional Capacity Assessment

Comprehensive assessment and detailed NDIS report writing to support funding decisions and identify the right level of support for your child.

How I Work

A Whole-Family Approach

My practice is grounded in three core values that guide everything I do.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family-Centred

You know your child best. I work in genuine partnership with parents and caregivers, ensuring goals are meaningful and strategies are practical for your family's life.

🎲

Play-Based

Play is how children learn. Sessions are engaging, joyful and purposeful — therapy that doesn't feel like therapy, happening in the places your child already loves.

🔬

Evidence-Informed

Every strategy I use is backed by current research and best practice. I continually upskill to ensure your child receives the most effective care available.
